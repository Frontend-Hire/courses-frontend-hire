# Introduction

Building in public is a great way to share your progress and get feedback from the community. It's also a great way to keep yourself accountable and motivated to finish your project.

But it can be hard to know where to start. What should you share? How often should you share? What if you don't have anything to share?

No matter how good your skills are, if you do not sell yourself, you might be missing out on a lot of potential. Building in public is a great way to sell yourself and your skills.

This guide will help you get started with building in public. It will give you some ideas for what to share, how to share it, and how to keep yourself accountable.

We will also focus on making your public sharing accounts look good. This is important because it will help you get more followers, engagements, and potential opportunities.

<Callout>
This guide is valid for almost all professions. Whether you're a developer, designer, marketer, or something else, you can use this guide to build in public. But we will focus on developers because that's what I know best.
</Callout>
